Welcome to OmniLocal Sandbox

WebLLM downloads and operates state-of-the-art Large Language Models directly on your graphics card (WebGPU) inside your browser. No remote APIs are contacted, making it fully private, free, and secure.

Quick Start Guide

  1. Pick a model size that matches your system hardware capacity (e.g. Qwen 2.5 0.5B for basic/mobile devices or Llama 3.2 1B for normal machines).
  2. Press the Initialize LLM button to fetch artifacts locally.
  3. Once loaded, start a zero-latency conversational sandbox chat!
